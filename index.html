ABOUT US

Interiors hub work with designers who specializes in incorporating innovative products and concepts that are simply stunning and often outshine the industry standards.
The designers come up with unique ideas for interior spaces of the clients and use various disciplines and elements of
interior design by focusing on practical application and implications of their concepts.

OUR DESIGN

Our designers are committed to design that creates clean lines and purity of space. Our services cover the entire construction process from start to finish; covering spatial planning, electrical and lighting layouts; ceiling and bulkhead designs for tender and construction purposes. This phase of the project also includes the specification or all finishes and fittings for the interior.

OUR DECORATIONS

Our designer ensure they work closely with our clients they effortlessly create high-end interiors exclusive to their own particular style and needs. Sourcing furniture and fabrics both locally and Internationally ensures that there are no limits to the what can be achieved. Our designers natural ability to design bespoke furniture items allows us to create finished interiors that are unique, sophisticated and timeless, exceeding all expectations.
